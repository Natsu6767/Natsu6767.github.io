Mohit Jain

Thank you for visiting my academic webpage! If you are interested in checking out my blog, click here.

I am a second year Computer Science Masters student at the University of California, San Diego, advised by Prof. Xiaolong Wang .

I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee in 2020, with major in Electrical Engineering. During Fall 2019 I spent a semester abroad at the University of Maryland, College Park doing research with Prof. Abhinav Shrivastava .

I am astounded by us humans’ abilities to adapt. We are the only species that can circumnavigate the world and now live even in the most remote corners of our beautiful planet. I believe that for machines and robots to work efficiently in our daily life they must be able to adapt fast and safely. I want to develop artificial intelligence methods that would allow robots to generalize and adapt to novel surroundings and continuously learn over their lifetime. My interests lie broadly at the intersection of Reinforcement Learning, Computer Vision, and Robotics.
